Yam

Yam is a very important common food crop in West Africa and one of the best especially when eaten with fried eggs.

Ingredients Measurement
Yam A tuber
Onion A small bulb
Table Salt A teaspoon
Water A bowl
Eggs 4 eggs
Tomato 4 tomatoes

Steps to take

  1. Cut off the ends of the yam and cut into slices
  2. Peel the slices and place in a bowl of water to prevent bowning
  3. Wash the yam slices under running water to remove dirt
  4. Place in a saucepan filled with water .The pot should not be too full
  5. Add a pinch of salt and cover the pot
  6. Place over medium heat and allow to cook for about 20mins
  7. Chop the onion and tomatoes
  8. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet
  9. Stir in chopped tomatoes and onions until coated
  10. Add Salt and flavors and let it cook for 3mins
  11. Slowly pour in the beaten eggs in a circular motion
  12. When cooked,set aside and let simmer
  13. Serve with Yam
